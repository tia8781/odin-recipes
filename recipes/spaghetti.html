Tasty Turkey Spaghetti

If you need a delicious, quick, and healthy meal for your family this is the perfect recipe. Your kids will never know that you swapped out ground beef for a heart healthy alternative!

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz package of pasta
  • pasta sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vegetable oil
  • 16 oz of ground turkey
  • 2 tablespoons of onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Begin to cook package of pasta.
  2. Add oil to pan and turn heat to medium.
  3. Put ground turkey in pan and break it up.
  4. Add spices to turkey and cook until light brown.
  5. Add ground turkey to pot of cooked and drained pasta.
  6. Heat pasta sauce in pan on low and add to pasta and ground turkey.
  7. Serve.
