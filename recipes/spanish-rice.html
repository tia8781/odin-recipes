Tasty Spanish Rice

Serve a dish that your kids will actually eat. This tasty Spanish rice entree will have your whole family coming back for seconds. Create a great dish that goes well with any Taco Tuesday extravaganza!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups of salsa

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add onions and stir until tender.
  3. Add rice and stir until rice starts to turn light brown.
  4. Stir in chicken broth and salsa.
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer.
  6. Cover and cook for about 15 minutes until liquid is absorbed.
  7. Remove pan from heat and let sit for 10 minutes.
