Collard Greens

Collard Greens with Bacon!

This recipe will wow at your next family gathering. The simplicity of collard greens combined with the deliciousness of bacon will make a dish no one will soon forget. Collard greens make the perfect savory side dish that is a Southern classic. Your guests will ask you for the recipe time and time again.

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs of rinsed collard greens
  • 6 slices of uncured bacon or regular bacon
  • 1 large, diced yellow onion
    • 6 cups of chicken stock
  • 6 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Rinse each collard green leaf until completely clean.
  2. Rip leaf away from the stem and dispose of the stem.
  3. Roll leaf and rip into bite-sized strips.
  4. Dice onion and chop garlic.
  5. Make chicken stock.
  6. Fry bacon until crisp in stock pot and remove bacon.
  7. Sautee onion in bacon grease for 5 minutes or until soft.
  8. Pour chicken stock into pot of sauteed onions.
  9. Add chopped garlic.
  10. Try a small amount of broth and season to taste if necessary.
  11. Put greens in pot, stir, and set stove to medium to simmer.
